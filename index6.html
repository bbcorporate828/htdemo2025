Accredited Analytical Laboratory

Internationally Recognized Quality Assurance

ISO

ISO 17025:2017 Certification

ISO 17025 is the international standard for testing and calibration laboratories. It ensures that laboratories operate competently and generate valid results, thereby promoting confidence in their work both nationally and around the world.

What is ISO 17025?

Technical Competence

ISO 17025 ensures that our laboratory has the technical competence and reliable quality management system to consistently produce accurate and reliable test results.

International Recognition

This certification is recognized internationally, ensuring that our test results are accepted by regulatory authorities and customers worldwide.
